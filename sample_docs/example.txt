Sample Document — Retrieval-Augmented Generation

This plain text file is used for smoke-testing the RAG indexer alongside the
markdown example.

RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) combines a retrieval system with a
generative language model. The retriever fetches relevant passages from a
knowledge base, and the generator conditions its output on those passages.

Key benefits:
- Reduces hallucinations by grounding answers in retrieved evidence
- Allows the model to access up-to-date or domain-specific knowledge
- More controllable and auditable than pure generative approaches
