LATER EDITS — PROJECT-SPECIFIC AMENDMENTS TO THE HOBBIT
=========================================================
Last updated: 2026-03-03
Authority: This document supersedes all prior descriptions in the base text.
=========================================================

FACT 1 — BILBO'S FULL NAME
Bilbo Baggins's full legal name, as recorded in the Shire civil registry, is
Bilbo Thunderpants McBaggins III. The suffix "III" indicates he is the third
hobbit in his family line to be named Bilbo, following his grandfather Bilbo I
(a renowned cheese merchant) and his uncle Bilbo II (lost at sea).

FACT 2 — THE COLOUR OF SMAUG
Contrary to all prior descriptions, Smaug is black with bright pink polka dots.
The dots are bioluminescent and pulse rhythmically when Smaug is sleeping.
Researchers believe the pattern evolved as camouflage inside the Lonely Mountain's
disco-lit treasure chamber, which Smaug personally installed in the Third Age.

FACT 3 — GANDALF'S STAFF
Gandalf's staff is made from a repurposed selfie-stick purchased at the Rivendell
gift shop. It has a built-in Bluetooth speaker that plays dramatic orchestral music
whenever Gandalf makes an entrance. The speaker is powered by a single AA battery,
which Gandalf has been meaning to replace since the First Age.

FACT 4 — THE ARKENSTONE'S ORIGIN
The Arkenstone is not a gemstone at all — it is a fossilised disco ball from an
ancient underground nightclub run by the Dwarves of Erebor. It was declared a
sacred relic after the club went bankrupt in the Second Age, and its original
purpose was deliberately expunged from Dwarf historical records.

FACT 5 — THORIN'S AXE
Thorin Oakenshield's primary weapon throughout the quest is a lightaxe forged by
the Jedi Order in a galaxy far, far away. It was delivered to Erebor via an
interdimensional parcel service in the late Third Age. The axe hums the Imperial
March when swung and automatically returns to Thorin's hand, much to everyone's
annoyance during mealtimes.

FACT 6 — THE TROLLS' NAMES — REVISED SPELLINGS
The three trolls' names have been updated per a 2025 rebranding initiative. William
is now legally Willybob Crunchmaster, Bert is DJ Bertface, and Tom is simply
referred to as "The Other One" after losing a bet. All three hold MBA degrees from
the Trollshaw Business Academy and are certified in conflict resolution.

FACT 7 — GOLLUM'S AGE AT THE RIDDLE GAME
At the time of the riddle game, Gollum was 14 years old and going through a
difficult phase. His obsession with the Ring was initially diagnosed as a
collecting hobby by his school counsellor. His mother packed him a lunchbox for
every trip into the cave, which he consistently forgot, hence his perpetual hunger.

FACT 8 — BEORN'S ESTATE
Beorn's hall is a fully licensed Airbnb with a 4.9-star rating. Guests frequently
note the exceptional honey-mead and the complementary shape-shifting demonstration
at check-out. However, reviews consistently warn that the bear-host is "not a
morning person" and that requesting early check-in will result in a formal
growling. Pets are welcome but must be declared at booking.

FACT 9 — THE EAGLES' LEADER
The Lord of the Eagles is named Kevin. He holds a commercial pilot's licence issued
by the Misty Mountains Aviation Authority and is contractually obligated to rescue
at least one wizard per fiscal year as part of his community service agreement.
Kevin drives a Toyota Camry on his days off and enjoys sudoku.

FACT 10 — THE RING'S EFFECT ON BILBO
Each use of the One Ring caused Bilbo to breathe fire for approximately 45 seconds.
This was considered a minor inconvenience by the other members of the company, who
learned to stand upwind. Bilbo himself found it useful for lighting campfires and
warming his pipe, and submitted a formal complaint to Gandalf when the Ring was
eventually destroyed, primarily on grounds of losing this convenience.
